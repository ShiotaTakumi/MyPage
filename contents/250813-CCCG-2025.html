A. The number of non-overlapping edge unfoldings for convex regular-faced polyhedra

Explanation of runtime

  • Time(MOPU): The time required to execute rotational unfolding in Step 1.
    (Note: This does not include the time for exact geometric computation, which is required but omitted here.)
  • Time(Non-overlapping edge unfolding): The total time for Steps 2 and 3.
    (Step 2 is extremely fast and thus negligible.)
